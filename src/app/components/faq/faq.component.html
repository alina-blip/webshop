FAQs

When will my order get shipped?

Most orders ship in 1 week. However, in some cases it can take up to 3-4 weeks before shipping. If you’d like to receive it urgently, please contact before placing an order.

Are prints signed by eevee?

Yes! All prints offered on this site are hand-signed and artist-approved. Limited edition prints are both signed and numbered.

Can I share your work on my blog, website or magazine?

Of course! I’m honored to be featured on your webpages or magazines. Please make sure to credit me as the author and link these images to my website. Thank you!

Which material do you use for traditional painting?

I paint mostly with acrylics, acrylagouache and aquarelle as well as pouring medium, pencils and aquarelle pencils.
